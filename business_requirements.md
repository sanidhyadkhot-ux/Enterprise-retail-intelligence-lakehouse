# Business Requirements

## Functional Requirements

- Ingest sales, inventory, supplier, product and customer datasets.
- Track daily revenue, product sales and inventory movement.
- Identify low-stock products.
- Calculate supplier lead time and fill rate.
- Produce Power BI-ready Gold tables.
- Generate demand forecasts and stockout predictions.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Scalable architecture
- Clean data validation
- Auditable raw Bronze layer
- Analytics-ready Gold layer
- Clear documentation
- Recruiter-friendly GitHub structure
