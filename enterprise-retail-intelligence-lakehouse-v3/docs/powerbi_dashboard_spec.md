# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Executive Overview

Cards:
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Gross Margin
- Inventory Value
- Stockout Risk Products

Charts:
- Daily Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Category
- Top Products
- Payment Method Split

## Inventory Dashboard

Cards:
- Low Stock Products
- Average Days of Inventory
- Total Stock on Hand

Charts:
- Stockout Risk by Product
- Inventory Value by Category
- Recommended Reorder Quantity

## Supplier Dashboard

Cards:
- Average Lead Time
- Supplier Fill Rate
- Late Delivery Count

Charts:
- Supplier Fill Rate Ranking
- Lead Time Trend
- Supplier Risk Matrix
